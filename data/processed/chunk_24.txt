You can build as many items and buy as many cards as 
you desire—as long as you have enough resources to “pay” for them and they are still available in the supply. (See Settlements Y , Cities Y , Roads Y , and Development Cards Y .)
Each player has a supply of 15 roads, 5 settlements, and 4 
cities. If you build a city, return the settlement to your supply. Roads and cities, however, remain on the board until the end of the game once they are built.
Your turn is over after “building,” and the player to your left 
continues the game.
