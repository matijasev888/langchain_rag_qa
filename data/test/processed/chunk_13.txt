a) Domestic Trade Y
On your turn, you can trade resource cards with any of the 
other players. You can announce which resources you need and 
what you are willing to trade for them. The other players can also make their own proposals and counteroffers.
Important: Players may only trade with the player whose turn it is. The other players may not trade among themselves.
