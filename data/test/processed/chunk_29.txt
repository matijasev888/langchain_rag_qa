desert
The desert is the only terrain hex that 
does not produce resources. The 
robber Y  starts the game there. 
A settlement or a city built adjacent to the desert yields fewer resources than those built next to one of the other terrain types.

cards: Knight Cards Y , Progress Cards Y , and 
Victory Point Cards Y .
When you buy a development card, take 
the top card of the draw pile into your hand. Keep your development cards hidden until you play them. This keeps the other players in the dark.
You cannot trade or give away development cards.
